How it works

Concept library: groups many phrasings of the same situation into one naval rewrite. Confidence mode: Safe only uses near-certain matches; Balanced is the default; Adventurous attempts broader rewrites. Event engine: recognises incidents such as falling overboard. Sentence engine: rewrites complete ideas naturally. Meaning engine: resolves ambiguous words from their surrounding context. Underneath those, the established exact phrase, synonym/context and dictionary layers remain in place. A full-book keyword index then suggests related source terms.

Example: “This curry is hot.” becomes “This scran's got a fair bit of reheat.” rather than treating “hot” as a context-free word.
